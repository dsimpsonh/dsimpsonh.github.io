Long bio.

15 Years · 4 Ventures · 2 Patents

I work at the seam where emerging technology meets commercial outcome — and where decisions become reality.

Today I’m Founder & CEO of Aletheai, building the UK’s first patent-pending, multi-signal behavioural prediction engine for estate agents. We’ve secured a strategic partnership and live pilot with Alto (Zoopla Group), unlocking distribution to 10,000+ agents and a projected £500K ARR in year one.

Before Aletheai I founded three other ventures — Monogram Publishers (independent publishing, since 2014), Shake Your Power (clean-tech education, US patent, two London Design Award Golds), and Glass Lab / Trash Surface Bureau (sustainable design, FRAME Magazine feature). I led design teams of 12+ at Slimming World’s 250K-member platform, served as Senior Lecturer at Nottingham Trent University, and have lectured, judged, mentored, and exhibited internationally — including at the V&A, Royal College of Art, European Commission, and London College of Communication.

I’m a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts, Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy, and Education Ambassador for CREATE. My writing lives on Substack. My playbook lives on Amazon.
